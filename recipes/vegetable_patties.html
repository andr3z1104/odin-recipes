Root Vegetable Patties

Description

Some left-over root vegetable mash can easily be turned into the most delicious patties the next day!

Ingredients

  • 400 g cold root vegetable mash
  • 3 chopped cloves of garlic
  • Chopped fresh thyme
  • Chopped fresh leaves from a parsley root
  • 1 egg
  • 4 tablespoons wholemeal flour

Steps

  1. Add garlic, thyme, root parsley leaves, egg, flour and salt to the vegetable mash and mix well. Season to taste and make sure the mixture is quite firm.
  2. Place spoonfuls of the mixture on a hot frying pan and cook the patties in butter until golden and crisp on both sides.
  3. Take the pot off the fire and place it close to the fire for 30 minutes to an hour.
  4. Serve these root vegetable patties with flatbread, fresh cream cheese and a tasty salad.
